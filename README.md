This is my first project : UnitWizard
It can convert a physical quantity like length,mass,temprature into its respective different unit.

Mapping(Key):
 
 For Physical Quantity:-
 1-->Length Conversion
 2-->Mass Conversion
 3-->Temprature Conversion

 For Units:-
 
  Length:-  1==>Kilometers , 
            2==>Meters , 
            3==>Centimeters
 
  Mass:- 1==>Kilograms , 
          2==>Grams , 
          3==>Milligrams
  
  Temprature:- 1==>Celsius , 
                2==>Fahrenheit , 
                3==>Kelvin
